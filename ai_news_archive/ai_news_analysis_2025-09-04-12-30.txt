- **Integration of Security Measures**: There is a clear trend towards integrating security measures directly into AI systems, particularly through frameworks that utilize information-flow control and dynamic monitoring.

- **Use of Multi-Agent Systems**: The use of multi-agent systems for both security and operational efficiency is becoming more prevalent, as seen in frameworks like LanternNet and RoboMemory.
