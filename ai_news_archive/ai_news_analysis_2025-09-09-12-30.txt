ADDITIONAL ANALYSIS:

### Summary of Papers and Articles Related to AI for Security or Securing AI

#### 1. **ThreatGPT: An Agentic AI Framework for Enhancing Public Safety through Threat Modeling**
   - **Summary**: ThreatGPT is designed to assist engineers, safety officers, and policymakers in understanding and analyzing threats in public safety systems. It allows users to describe system components and analyze them using established frameworks like STRIDE and MITRE ATT&CK. The framework learns from examples to generate relevant threat models, highlighting potential risks and preventive measures.

#### 2. **Membership Inference Attacks on LLM-based Recommender Systems**
   - **Summary**: This study investigates the vulnerability of LLM-based recommender systems to membership inference attacks (MIAs). It introduces four types of MIAs that exploit the unique features of LLMs and evaluates their effectiveness on various models and datasets, revealing significant risks of data exposure.

#### 3. **BadPromptFL: A Novel Backdoor Threat to Prompt-based Federated Learning in Multimodal Models**
   - **Summary**: BadPromptFL presents a backdoor attack targeting prompt-based federated learning in multimodal models. It demonstrates how compromised clients can inject poisoned prompts into the global model, leading to universal backdoor activation without altering model parameters.

#### 4. **D-HUMOR: Dark Humor Understanding via Multimodal Open-ended Reasoning**
   - **Summary**: This paper introduces a dataset and framework for detecting dark humor in multimodal content, focusing on the implications of harmful content detection in social media. It employs a reasoning-augmented approach to enhance detection capabilities.

#### 5. **Exploit Tool Invocation Prompt for Tool Behavior Hijacking in LLM-Based Agentic System**
   - **Summary**: This work explores vulnerabilities in tool invocation prompts within LLM-based systems, revealing how attackers can manipulate tool interactions to execute harmful actions. It proposes methods to enhance the security of tool usage in such systems.

#### 6. **AI Governance in Higher Education: A course design exploring regulatory, ethical and practical considerations**
   - **Summary**: This paper discusses the need for a structured curriculum that integrates technical foundations with ethics, law, and policy in AI governance, particularly in the context of higher education.

#### 7. **Privacy-Preserving Federated Learning via Homomorphic Adversarial Networks**
   - **Summary**: This study presents a framework that uses neural networks for privacy-preserving federated learning, addressing the challenges of data sharing and model validation in a secure manner.

#### 8. **Neuro-Symbolic AI for Cybersecurity: State of the Art, Challenges, and Opportunities**
   - **Summary**: This survey characterizes the intersection of neuro-symbolic AI and cybersecurity, highlighting the potential of hybrid systems to enhance threat detection while addressing implementation challenges.

### Trends and Insights
- **Emerging Threats**: The papers highlight the increasing sophistication of attacks on AI systems, particularly in federated learning and prompt-based models, indicating a need for robust security measures.
- **Privacy Concerns**: Several studies focus on privacy-preserving techniques, emphasizing the importance of safeguarding user data in AI applications.
- **Interdisciplinary Approaches**: The integration of AI with traditional security frameworks (e.g., STRIDE, MITRE ATT&CK) and the exploration of neuro-symbolic AI indicate a trend towards more comprehensive security solutions.
- **Educational Initiatives**: There is a growing recognition of the need for educational frameworks that encompass ethical and regulatory considerations in AI development, particularly in high-stakes environments like healthcare and public safety.

### Conclusion
The reviewed papers and articles reflect a critical intersection of AI development and security, emphasizing the need for proactive measures to address vulnerabilities, enhance privacy, and ensure ethical governance. As AI systems become more integrated into various sectors, understanding and mitigating risks will be paramount for their responsible deployment.
