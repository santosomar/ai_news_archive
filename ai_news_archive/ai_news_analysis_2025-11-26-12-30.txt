- [Using Wearable Devices to Improve Chronic Pain Treatment among Patients with Opioid Use Disorder](https://arxiv.org/abs/2511.19577): This paper explores the potential of wearable devices combined with AI to monitor chronic pain and opioid use disorder, finding that machine learning models can predict pain spikes effectively.
