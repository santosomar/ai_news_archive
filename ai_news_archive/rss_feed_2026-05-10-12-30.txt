Summary: <p>Three weeks into testing, a learner told me my AI tutor gave her the wrong answer.</p>
<p>Not obviously wrong — just outdated enough to mislead.</p>
<p>That was the moment I realized something most RAG systems quietly ignore: they have no sense of time. My system retrieved the most similar document, not the most current one. And in a knowledge base that changes constantly, that’s a serious flaw.</p>
