   - "Governance and Regulation of Artificial Intelligence in Developing Countries" discusses the need for context-specific regulatory frameworks to manage the ethical implications of AI, particularly in regions with limited oversight.

   - "FedDetox" introduces a framework for on-device data sanitization to maintain safety alignment in federated learning, showcasing the need for robust mechanisms to handle toxic or unsafe information.
