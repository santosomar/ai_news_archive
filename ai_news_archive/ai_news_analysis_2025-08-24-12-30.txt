- [Mobile-Agent-v3: Foundamental Agents for GUI Automation](https://tldr.takara.ai/p/2508.15144): This paper presents GUI-Owl and Mobile-Agent-v3, open-source GUI agent models that achieve state-of-the-art performance across various benchmarks through innovations in environment infrastructure and scalable reinforcement learning.
- [LiveMCP-101: Stress Testing and Diagnosing MCP-enabled Agents on Challenging Queries](https://tldr.takara.ai/p/2508.15760): LiveMCP-101 benchmarks AI agents' abilities to use multiple tools in real-world scenarios, revealing significant challenges in tool orchestration and inefficiencies in token usage.
- [Deep Think with Confidence](https://tldr.takara.ai/p/2508.15260): DeepConf enhances reasoning efficiency in LLMs by filtering low-quality reasoning traces using model-internal confidence signals, achieving high accuracy and reducing token generation.
- [Waver: Wave Your Way to Lifelike Video Generation](https://tldr.takara.ai/p/2508.15761): Waver is a high-performance foundation model for generating videos and images, excelling in text-to-video, image-to-video, and text-to-image tasks using a Hybrid Stream DiT architecture.
- [SceneGen: Single-Image 3D Scene Generation in One Feedforward Pass](https://tldr.takara.ai/p/2508.15769): SceneGen generates multiple 3D assets from a single image using a novel framework that integrates local and global scene information, enabling efficient 3D content creation.
- [aiXiv: A Next-Generation Open Access Ecosystem for Scientific Discovery](https://tldr.takara.ai/p/2508.15126): aiXiv is an open-access platform that facilitates the submission, review, and refinement of scientific proposals and papers by both human and AI scientists, enhancing the quality of AI-generated research.
- [A Survey on Large Language Model Benchmarks](https://tldr.takara.ai/p/2508.15361): This paper reviews large language model benchmarks, identifying issues such as data contamination and cultural biases, and proposes a design paradigm for future improvements.
- [ATLAS: Decoupling Skeletal and Shape Parameters for Expressive Parametric Human Modeling](https://tldr.takara.ai/p/2508.15767): ATLAS is a high-fidelity body model that improves pose accuracy and shape expressivity by decoupling shape and skeleton parameters using extensive training data.
- [Visual Autoregressive Modeling for Instruction-Guided Image Editing](https://tldr.takara.ai/p/2508.15772): VAREdit improves image editing adherence and efficiency by using a Scale-Aligned Reference module to condition source image tokens, outperforming diffusion-based methods.
- [Does the cafe entrance look accessible? Where is the door? Towards Geospatial AI Agents for Visual Inquiries](https://tldr.takara.ai/p/2508.15752): Geo-Visual Agents integrate geospatial images and GIS data to answer nuanced visual-spatial inquiries about the world.
- [Snap-Snap: Taking Two Images to Reconstruct 3D Human Gaussians in Milliseconds](https://tldr.takara.ai/p/2508.14892): This method reconstructs 3D human bodies from two sparse views using a redesigned geometry model, achieving state-of-the-art performance.
- [LLaSO: A Foundational Framework for Reproducible Research in Large Language and Speech Model](https://tldr.takara.ai/p/2508.15418): LLaSO is an open framework for large-scale speech-language modeling that enhances reproducibility and performance through alignment data and evaluation benchmarks.
- [When and What: Diffusion-Grounded VideoLLM with Entity Aware Segmentation for Long Video Understanding](https://tldr.takara.ai/p/2508.15641): Grounded VideoDiT addresses temporal perception and entity interaction in videos, achieving state-of-the-art results on VideoQA benchmarks.
- [Fin-PRM: A Domain-Specialized Process Reward Model for Financial Reasoning in Large Language Models](https://tldr.takara.ai/p/2508.15202): Fin-PRM enhances intermediate reasoning in LLMs for finance through step-level and trajectory-level supervision, improving performance across various applications.
- [INTIMA: A Benchmark for Human-AI Companionship Behavior](https://tldr.takara.ai/p/2508.09998): INTIMA evaluates companionship behaviors in language models, revealing differences in handling emotional support and boundary-setting.
