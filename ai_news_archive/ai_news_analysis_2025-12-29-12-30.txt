### Summary of AI Papers and News Articles Related to Security

#### 1. **LAMLAD: Feature-Level Adversarial Attacks on Android Malware Detectors**
   - **Summary**: This paper presents LAMLAD, a framework that utilizes large language models (LLMs) to create adversarial attacks on Android malware detection systems. It highlights the effectiveness of generating realistic feature perturbations to evade detection while maintaining malicious functionality. The framework achieves a high attack success rate and proposes a defense strategy to enhance robustness against such attacks.

#### 2. **Reflection-Driven Control for Trustworthy Code Agents**
   - **Summary**: This paper introduces Reflection-Driven Control, a module for LLM agents that enhances safety by incorporating self-reflection into the decision-making process. It aims to prevent harmful outputs by monitoring the agent's reasoning and retrieving secure coding guidelines when risks are detected, thus improving the security of code generation tasks.

#### 3. **X-Boundary: Establishing Exact Safety Boundary to Shield LLMs from Multi-Turn Jailbreaks**
   - **Summary**: The study proposes X-Boundary, a method to protect LLMs from multi-turn jailbreaks by establishing a clear boundary between safe and harmful representations. It demonstrates significant improvements in defense performance while maintaining usability, addressing the challenge of balancing safety and functionality in AI systems.

#### 4. **Mitigating LLM Hallucination via Behaviorally Calibrated Reinforcement Learning**
