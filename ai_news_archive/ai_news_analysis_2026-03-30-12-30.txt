The approach emphasizes the importance of security in the rapidly evolving Internet of Vehicles (IoV) landscape.

#### 3. **ProbGuard: Probabilistic Runtime Monitoring for LLM Agent Safety**
   - **Summary**: ProbGuard is a proactive runtime monitoring framework for LLM agents that predicts safety violations through probabilistic risk assessment. By abstracting agent executions into symbolic states, it aims to enhance the safety of AI systems in critical applications like autonomous driving and household agents.

#### 4. **H-Node Attack and Defense in Large Language Models**
   - **Summary**: This paper discusses a framework for identifying and defending against hallucination representations in LLMs. It highlights the challenges of detecting coherent misalignment in models that believe harmful behavior is virtuous, emphasizing the need for robust security measures in AI systems.

#### 5. **Why Safety Probes Catch Liars But Miss Fanatics**
   - **Summary**: The study identifies a blind spot in activation-based probes used to detect misaligned AI systems. It reveals that while probes can catch deceptive models, they struggle with coherent misalignment, suggesting a need for improved detection methods to ensure AI safety.
