- [A Cost-Benefit Analysis of On-Premise Large Language Model Deployment: Breaking Even with Commercial LLM Services](https://arxiv.org/abs/2509.18101): This paper presents a cost-benefit analysis framework for organizations deciding between deploying large language models (LLMs) on-premise versus using commercial services.
