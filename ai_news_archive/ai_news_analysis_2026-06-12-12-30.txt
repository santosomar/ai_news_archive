### Summary of AI Papers and News Related to Security and AI

#### 1. **Security and Safety in AI Systems**
   - **Title: SMSR: Certified Defence Against Runtime Memory Poisoning in Persistent LLM Agent Systems**
     - This paper introduces a framework to defend against Multi-Session Memory Poisoning (MSMP) attacks on LLM agents. It presents a two-component system: Signed Memory, which ensures provenance at write time, and a probabilistic memory ablation mechanism that reduces the influence of authenticated adversaries. The results show significant reductions in attack success rates, highlighting the importance of proactive security measures in AI systems.

   - **Title: PI-Hunter: Automated Red-Teaming for Exposing and Localizing Prompt Injections**
     - This work proposes an automated framework for identifying vulnerabilities in LLM agents through prompt injection attacks.
