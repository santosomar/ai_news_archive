- **Dynamic Adaptation**: AI systems must adapt to changing user preferences and contexts, which requires robust mechanisms for understanding and aligning with human values.
- **Robustness Against Attacks**: There is a critical need for AI systems to withstand adversarial attacks and maintain performance under various conditions, particularly in high-stakes environments.
- **Ethical and Privacy Considerations**: As AI systems become more integrated into everyday life, ensuring data privacy and ethical behavior is paramount. This includes developing frameworks for evaluating AI behavior in real-world contexts.
- **Generative Model Challenges**: While generative models show promise in various applications, ensuring their outputs are reliable and safe remains a significant challenge.
- **Human-AI Collaboration**: Designing AI systems that assist and enhance human decision-making processes is becoming increasingly important, moving beyond traditional user-agent interactions.
